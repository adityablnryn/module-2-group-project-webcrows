Game developed by team Webcrows !

Othello is a strategy game played by two players: Black and White. It is played on an 8x8 board, called the Othello board. The two players place 64 discs, each of which is black on one side and black on the other. For convenience, each player begins with 32 discs but these do not belong to him and if his opponent runs out of discs, he is obliged to give him some. A disc is black if the black side is visible and orange if the orange face is on top.
